Our Cookie Policy

Our website places cookies that we believe are necessary to get the best out of our website. Cookies are small text files that a webpage places on a computer, tablet or mobile phone. These cookies are used to ensure the web pages of OSBO functions properly, and to monitor the site's activity. We only use this data in an aggregated way, and it cannot be traced back to a computer or individual. You can disable cookies through the settings of your browser (i.e. Firefox, Chrome, Safari, etc.).
