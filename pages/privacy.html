PRIVACY AND SECURITY

Obso Ltd. is committed to protecting your privacy. The amount and type of information we collect from you depends on your activities and use of our Website. Certain services offered on or through the Website require you to register and/or provide information that personally identifies you ("personal and account information") for purposes of interacting with our Website. In this policy, "personal and account information" means your name, company name, account number, address, telephone number, and e-mail address plus any other information that personally identifies you, including your industry, purchase history, and IP address, or would permit us to contact you. This policy describes our practices regarding personal and account information collected through our Website.

Marketing Communications

With your explicit consent, we may use your email address to send you marketing communications about our products, services, promotions, and other relevant updates. You have the right to withdraw your consent for marketing purposes at any time by clicking the 'unsubscribe' link provided in our marketing emails or by contacting us directly. Your withdrawal of consent will not affect the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal.

When You Browse

We may collect your IP address, referral website, your browser type, and monitor your movements throughout our Website. This information helps us provide you with a more personalised experience.

When You Register

We collect your username, password, e-mail address, security question, and answer to your security question, to protect your Account. We use your security question and answer to identify you as the account user if you have forgotten your password. You can help us ensure your privacy by not sharing your username and/or password.

When You Purchase

We collect your name, address and payment information to process your order. However, you may provide credit card and purchase order numbers by telephone. We also collect your IP address and information contained in "cookies," such as your purchases and login data, to personalize your shopping experience. Your browser must be set to accept cookies if you wish to add items to the shopping cart.

When you communicate with us through our Website we collect your e-mail address and the other information that you provide to respond to your communication.

How Can I Exercise My Rights?

You have the right to inspect your personal information, to correct or remove it. In addition, you have the right to withdraw any permission for data processing or to object to the processing of your data. Finally, you have the right to data transfer. That means that you can submit a request with us to send the personal data that we hold on you, in a computer file to you or any other party. Of course, we are at all times held to legal compliance relating to the storage and provision of data and information.
